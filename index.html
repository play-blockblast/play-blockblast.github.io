Getting Started with Block Blast

Game Basics

Jump right into the action with Block Blast's intuitive gameplay mechanics:

  • Tap or click on groups of 2 or more matching blocks to clear them
  • Create larger matches for higher score multipliers
  • Watch for cascading effects as blocks fall into empty spaces
  • Plan your moves strategically to trigger chain reactions
  • Complete level objectives to progress through the game

The more blocks you match at once, the higher your score will climb!

Special Power-ups & Boosters

Master the art of power-ups to dominate the game! Match 4 or more blocks to create these powerful special blocks:

Rainbow Block

Clears all blocks of one color from the board. Perfect for creating massive chain reactions!

Bomb Block

Creates a powerful explosion clearing blocks in a 3x3 area. Ideal for tight spots!

Lightning Block

Zaps an entire row or column clear of blocks. Great for reaching difficult areas!

Super Combo Block

Combine power-ups for spectacular chain reactions and massive point bonuses!
